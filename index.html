Fight enemies, defeat the boss, and escape alive.
Star Brawler 2 is a game made for the Raylib Game Jam.
The theme of the jam is "That was close", so we made a melee space shooter, again.
How to play:
Arrow keys to move.
A - Basic attack
S - Laser
D - Flare attack
F - BFL
Your main combat tool is the counterfield, the green sphere surronding your ship. The counterfield can absorb energy from enemy's attacks that passes through it's radius. The ship's energy is both your life bar and the power source used by your special weapons: Laser, Flare, and B.F.L. Manage your ship's energy through the Auric armada, destroy their mother ship, and make it out before being engulfed by the golden flames!
